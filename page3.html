Philippines Edition

Welcome to the Philippines, a beautiful country rich in nature and history! With stunning beaches, lush landscapes, and historical sites, it's an ideal place to explore. Experience warm hospitality, diverse culture, and create lasting memories in this captivating destination. Come, discover the Philippines — where beauty and history make every journey unforgettable!

Magellan's Cross

- This cross is a must-see monument, as it is one of the most iconic historical landmarks in Cebu City. Magellan's Cross is a reminder of how Christianity was brought to the Philippines.

Ariel's Point

- In Boracay, it is known for its 15-meter high "diving boards" where adventure junkies can leap off into the clear water. Even if you're not up for the thrill, this area has one of the clearest waters and richest marine life in Boracay.

Chocolate Hills

- These hills are the Philippines' third National Geographical Monument, located on the island of Bohol. They also look like chocolate truffles due to their rounded hills appearance that turns brown during the dry season.
