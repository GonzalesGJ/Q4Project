CHARACTERS IN NOLI ME TANGERE
Juan Crisóstomo Ibarra y Magsalin Crisostomo Ibarra is the main character in the story. He's from the Philippines and has come back to his hometown after studying in Europe. He's the son of Don Rafael Ibarra, and he's a noble and well-educated person.
Maria Clara Maria Clara is the daughter of Kapitan Tiago, and she's the one Crisostomo Ibarra is in love with. She's a gentle and kind young woman, famous for her beauty and elegance. Her character symbolizes purity and innocence.
Sisa Sisa is the mother of Basilio and Crispin. She goes through a lot of difficulties that show how the common people suffered during the colonial period. She's a loving and obedient wife to Pedro, the father of her children, and she still cares for him even though he neglects and mistreats her.
Basilio Basilio is Crispin's older brother, and a son of Sisa and Pedro. He stays positive and hopeful despite being deeply affected by the injustices faced by his family.
Crispin Crispin is Basilio's younger brother and the son of Sisa and Pedro. He's just a young boy who was falsely accused of stealing in the local church, which led to him suffering from unfair treatment and abuse.
